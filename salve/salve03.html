Salve Regina part 3

Today we will look at the third part of Salve Regina

Eia ergo, advocata nostra,

illos tuos misericordes oculos ad nos converte.

Eia
Come! or Ha! or Ah! or Come on! - also crops up in Latin Christmas Carols.
ergo
Therefore - very handy to use in explanations. State all your reasons, dramatic pause, "Ergo..." then give your conclusion. For extra cleverness add "Quod erat demonstrandum."
advocata
advocate - someone who speaks on our behalf
nostra
our - remember noster, nostra, nostrum for masculine, feminine and neuter
illos
those
tuos
thine
misericordes
merciful
oculos
eyes
ad
to
nos
us
converte
turn or convert
