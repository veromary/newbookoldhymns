A medieval four part piece for Christmas.

<div data-gloss>
<p>Gaudete! Gaudete! Christus est natus ex Maria Virgine! Gaudete</p>
<p>Gaudete! Gaudete! Christus est natus ex Maria Virgine! Gaudete</p>
<p>Rejoice! Rejoice! Christ is born of Mary Virgin! Rejoice!</p>
<p>Rejoice! Rejoice! Christ is born of the Virgin Mary! Rejoice</p>
</div>

<div data-gloss>
<p>Tempus adest gratiae, hoc quod optabamus, carmina laetitiae, devotes redamus.</p>
<p>Tempus adest gratiae, hoc quod optabamus, carmina laetitiae, devotes redamus.</p>
<p>Time arrives grace, this that {we wished}, song {of joy}, devoutly {we render}</p>
<p>The time of grace has come, for which we have prayed, let us devoutly sing songs of joy.</p>
</div>
