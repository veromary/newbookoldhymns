Sing All the Easter Music!

We start with a relatively modern piece, only a couple of hundred years old, then a medieval sequence and finish with a piece from around the fall of the Roman Empire.

  • O Filii et Filiae
  • Alleluia - the really famous one
  • Victimae Paschali Laudes
  • Salve Festa Dies

All these pieces and more are in newbookoldhymns.brandt.id.au. Download and print your own or buy a printed copy!
