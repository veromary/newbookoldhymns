We'll have to record some pick up episodes to cover the Feast of the Holy Name of Jesus and Candlemas and Lent and Passiontide. For now let's go with Easter.

  • Haec Dies
  • Jubilate Deo (a round attributed to Michael Praetorius)
  • Regina Caeli
